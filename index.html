Sabaism (worship of stars)

In the safe, soft, quiet night there is something so majestical
So pure
It's the millions and billions of little dots
Known as stars to mankind
Their so far away and yet they feel like I am home
They will listen and I will continue talking
Forever talking to my friends
They were here before me and they will be after I took my last breath
For them, I am just another human being
Passing by in a milli second
But for me, they are the whole universe
Lovely companions on my way through life
I am forever thankful for the view I have at night
And for the feeling they give me
I can't imagine something more magical
